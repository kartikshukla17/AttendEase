Instructions for Using the Attendance Management Component

For Event Organizers:

1. Generate the Form: - Enter your email address to create a new attendance form. - You will receive edit access to the form through the email you provided. 2. Customize the Form: - Open the form link received via email. - You can edit the form to suit your event’s needs but **do not remove the USN field**, as it is critical for tracking. 3. Share the QR Code: - Generate the QR code for the form. - Share the QR code with students, either by displaying it during the event or sending it via email or messaging apps. 4. Collect Attendance Data: - Students will scan the QR code and fill in the form during the event. - Submitted data will automatically populate a linked Google Sheet, which you will also have access to. 5. Update Attendance for Proctors: - If you wish to share the data with proctors, click the Update Attendance button. - This will distribute attendance data to the respective proctors' sheets, ensuring they have updated records. 6. End-of-Semester Summary: - At the end of the semester, proctors will have a consolidated record of all the events attended by their assigned students. Key Points to Remember: - Ensure your email is correctly entered during form generation to receive edit access. - Always keep the **USN field** in the form intact for accurate student tracking. - Use the **Update Attendance** button responsibly to keep proctors' records up-to-date. This system is designed to streamline event attendance tracking while maintaining an organized record for both organizers and proctors.
